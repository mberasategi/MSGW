In-class activity

History of a media outlet

In groups,

  1. Choose a media outlet (local newspaper, magazine of interest…)
  2. Go to the WaybackMachine:
    • Type in the web address of your site of choice
    • By clicking on the years displayed, build up a picture of how many pages, multimedia features and designs the site has had
    • Can you chart its major turning points? For example, when it started to add interactive features, or user-generated content, a responsive design…
  3. Choose the most relevant screenshots to briefly present the evolution of your site to the class
