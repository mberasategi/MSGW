Set up your HTML&CSS
learning environment

    • Leave your profile visible for everyone
  2. Follow the HTML & CSS track
  3. Let me know about your username
  4. Check your position in the Codecademy Ranking 2018

How this is going to be assessed

  • You will use these skills for A4
  • Finishing the HTML & CSS track in Codecademy is compulsory (if you don’t, you will lose marks in your grade for A4)
  • You can optionally follow other courses on web development such as Make a website or Make an interactive website, or even learn other languages such as JavaScript, PHP or Python. Finishing those will give you extra marks in your final individual grade.
